Hydrangea Haven

★★★★☆ (4.3)

₹399

Beautiful blue hydrangeas paired with fresh greenery create a bouquet representing gratitude, understanding, and heartfelt emotions. A lovely gift for family and close friends.

Care Instructions

  • Trim stems by 1 inch before placing in water.
  • Use fresh clean water every 2 days.
  • Keep away from direct sunlight.
  • Avoid placing near fruits.
  • Store in a cool room for longer freshness.
