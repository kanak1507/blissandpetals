Lily Elegance

★★★★☆ (4.8)

₹659

Graceful white lilies wrapped in luxurious paper create an elegant floral arrangement symbolizing purity, peace, and refined beauty. Ideal for weddings and special celebrations.

Care Instructions

  • Trim stems by 1 inch before placing in water.
  • Use fresh clean water every 2 days.
  • Keep away from direct sunlight.
  • Avoid placing near fruits.
  • Store in a cool room for longer freshness.
