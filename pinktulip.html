Pink Tulip Dream

★★★★★ (4.9)

₹409

Elegant pink tulips arranged with fresh foliage create a bouquet full of affection and happiness. A timeless choice for birthdays, anniversaries, and heartfelt surprises.

Care Instructions

  • Trim stems by 1 inch before placing in water.
  • Use fresh clean water every 2 days.
  • Keep away from direct sunlight.
  • Avoid placing near fruits.
  • Store in a cool room for longer freshness.
