Peony Romance

★★★★☆ (4.8)

₹689

Delicate peonies in soft pastel shades create an enchanting bouquet filled with romance and elegance. Perfect for anniversaries, weddings, and heartfelt expressions of love.

Care Instructions

  • Trim stems by 1 inch before placing in water.
  • Use fresh clean water every 2 days.
  • Keep away from direct sunlight.
  • Avoid placing near fruits.
  • Store in a cool room for longer freshness.
