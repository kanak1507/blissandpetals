Golden Dawn Bouquet

★★★★☆ (4.6)

₹379

Radiant yellow daffodils celebrate hope, prosperity, and fresh beginnings. This bouquet is perfect for housewarming parties, graduations, and uplifting gifts.

Care Instructions

  • Trim stems by 1 inch before placing in water.
  • Use fresh clean water every 2 days.
  • Keep away from direct sunlight.
  • Avoid placing near fruits.
  • Store in a cool room for longer freshness.
