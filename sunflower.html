Bright golden sunflowers paired with lush greenery bring warmth and happiness to every moment. A cheerful bouquet designed to brighten someone's day and spread positive energy.

Care Instructions

  • Trim stems by 1 inch before placing in water.
  • Use fresh clean water every 2 days.
  • Keep away from direct sunlight.
  • Avoid placing near fruits.
  • Store in a cool room for longer freshness.
